Section 3: The Operon Pitch Framework

1. Opening Hook (30 seconds)

  • Ask about their rep turnover
  • Connect with their pain immediately
  • Establish your credibility (30+ years in industry)

2. Pain Point (1 minute)

  • Describe the problems in vivid detail
  • Use their language and industry terms
  • Quantify the cost of inaction

3. Solution Overview (30 seconds)

  • Introduce the three-product ecosystem
  • Emphasize integration and completeness

4. Product Demos (2-3 minutes)

  • APEX Index: Hiring smarter
  • Vida Rep Academy: Training faster
  • Operon AI: Closing more

5. Close (30 seconds)

  • Offer the special ARDA deal
  • Get contact information
  • Set up next steps

Section 5: Handling Objections

Common Objections & Responses:

"We already have a training program."

Response: "That's great. How do you track progress? Can you see exactly where each rep is in their training? Our admin dashboard gives you complete visibility and ensures consistent quality across all reps."

"This seems expensive."

Response: "Consider the cost of one bad hire — $15,000+. APEX helps you avoid that entirely. The ROI is 13,000% when you close just 2 extra deals per month."

"We don't have time to implement new technology."

Response: "That's exactly why we built it. It's designed to save time, not add to it. Once set up, Operon runs automatically, Vida training happens online, and APEX screens candidates before you even interview them."

"My team won't adopt AI."

Response: "Our voice commands make AI accessible. Reps just talk to it like a person. No complex interfaces to learn. It's designed to make their job easier, not harder."
