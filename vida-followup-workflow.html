7 Automated Touchpoints

📋
Point of Sale
Digital Intake + Contract Scan
Rep fills a simple form on their phone or tablet. Name, email, phone, package, price, resort. Then photos the contract — AI extracts all data automatically.
📧
Hour 1
Personalized Welcome Email
Automated email sent FROM the rep's own email address. Warm, personal, feels hand-written.
"Hi Maria! I'm so excited you joined the Vida family today. Here's everything you need to get started..."
📞
Day 1
Voice Welcome Call (Rep's Cloned Voice)
Twilio calls the client automatically. ElevenLabs plays the rep's cloned voice — sounds exactly like them.
"Hi Maria! It's Carlos from Vida Vacations — just wanted to personally welcome you to the family!"
🤖
Ongoing
AI FAQ Autoresponder (24/7)
Clients text or call with questions — AI answers instantly, 24/7. Rep only gets pinged if someone is upset.
📬
Days 1-7
7-Day Email Nurture Sequence
Automated emails from rep's address every few days. Welcome, booking tips, benefits, destinations.
📞
Day 30
Check-In Call
Rep's cloned voice checks in automatically. Unhappy signals → manager alerted immediately.
🎁
Day 45
Referral Ask
Happy member? Perfect time to ask. Personalized text + email with referral link.
